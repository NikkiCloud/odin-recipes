Old-Fashioned Pink Lemonade

Description

This gorgeous pink lemonade recipe is the perfect way to cool down on a hot summer day.

Serve the pink lemonade over ice and garnish each glass with a lemon slice or wedge for an extra pop of color.

Ingredients

  • 9 cups water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup cranberry juice, chilled
  • ice as needed

Steps

  1. Combine water, sugar, lemon juice, and cranberry juice in a large pitcher; stir until sugar dissolves.
  2. Serve in tall glasses over ice.
